Common Questions

Pricing FAQs.

Do you charge more for larger vehicles?

Yes — SUVs and crossovers add $30–$50. Full-size trucks and large SUVs add $50–$80. Your exact quote is confirmed at booking, before any work begins.

Is there an upfront deposit?

No. We don't take payment until the job is complete and you're satisfied. We believe the work should speak before the invoice does.

What if my car is in worse condition than expected?

We assess your vehicle on arrival. If additional work is needed, we'll tell you before we start — never after. You approve any changes before we proceed.

Not sure which programme you need?

Select "Help Me Choose" on the booking form. We'll ask a few questions about your vehicle and recommend the right programme before confirming anything.
